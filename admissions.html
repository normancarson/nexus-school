Get Started

How to Apply

Becoming an NIIT facilitated student is simple. Follow these steps to begin your international degree journey.

1. Apply

Complete the online application below with your details and program interest.

2. Submit Documents

Upload academic transcripts, ID, and a short statement of purpose.

3. Advisory Call

Meet your academic advisor to confirm the best international pathway for you.

4. Enroll & Begin

Pay enrollment, attend orientation, and start your hybrid learning journey.

Enrollment Process

What Happens After You Apply

Our admissions team will guide you step-by-step through enrollment in your chosen international online program, plus the NIIT facilitation package.

  • Document verification — We review your records within 5 business days
  • Program matching — Advisor confirms university and pathway
  • University enrollment — We help you register with the international partner
  • NIIT enrollment — Sign your facilitation agreement and pay fees
  • Orientation invite — Receive your campus orientation schedule

Fees & Scholarships

Transparent, Flexible & Accessible

Affordable monthly or per-semester fees that cover all NIIT services — mentorship, AI lab access, study environment and project coaching.

Eligibility

Student Requirements

To enroll in an NIIT facilitated international degree pathway, applicants should meet the following requirements:

  • Completed secondary school (A-Level / equivalent) for Bachelor's pathways
  • Bachelor's degree for Master's pathways
  • Functional English communication skills
  • Basic computer literacy (we'll help you build digital skills further)
  • Reliable access to internet (NIIT campus provides on-site)
  • Commitment to attend scheduled facilitation sessions
  • Valid National ID or Passport
