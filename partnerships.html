Stronger Together

Building a Network for Student Success

NIIT collaborates with leading technology providers, accredited international learning platforms, employers, and innovation hubs to deliver world-class outcomes.

01 — Technology Partners

Powered by Industry-Leading Tools

We partner with leading AI, cloud, and software companies to give our students access to the best learning tools available.

  • AI tool providers (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini ecosystems)
  • Cloud computing & developer platforms
  • Productivity & collaboration tool licenses
  • Free or discounted student software access
02 — Employer Networks

Real Pathways to Real Jobs

NIIT works with local and international employers to connect students with internships, projects, and career opportunities.

  • Internship placements with partner companies
  • Real client projects for portfolio building
  • Career fairs and recruiter days
  • Alumni network and referrals
03 — Learning Platforms

Accredited Online Universities

NIIT facilitates enrollment with accredited international online universities and high-quality MOOC platforms across multiple disciplines.

  • Accredited international online universities
  • Industry-recognized certification platforms
  • Specialized MOOC providers
  • Professional skills platforms
04 — Innovation Collaborations

Connected to Rwanda's Innovation Ecosystem

We collaborate with incubators, accelerators, and innovation hubs in Rwanda and beyond to plug our students into the broader ecosystem.

  • Innovation hub and incubator collaborations
  • Hackathon and competition partnerships
  • Joint research and innovation projects
  • Startup mentorship and seed connections
