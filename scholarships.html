Scholarships & Financial Support

Home  /  Scholarships

Education for All

Investing in Rwanda's Future Leaders

NIIT believes financial limitations should never stop a determined learner. Our scholarship programs are designed to make international degree pathways accessible to every qualified student.

01 — Merit Scholarships

Reward for Excellence

Top-performing applicants with outstanding academic records, leadership experience, or innovation potential are eligible for our Merit Scholarship awards.

  • Up to 100% facilitation fee waivers
  • Awarded based on transcripts and interviews
  • Renewable each semester upon performance
  • Special recognition awards for top scholars
02 — Need-Based Support

Helping Students Who Need It Most

For talented students from low-income backgrounds, NIIT offers need-based partial scholarships and flexible payment plans.

  • Family income & circumstance review
  • Partial fee waivers
  • Extended installment plans
  • Optional work-study opportunities on campus
03 — Community Support

For Community Leaders & Innovators

Awarded to students with a track record of community impact, civic engagement, or social entrepreneurship.

  • Recognizes social impact and volunteerism
  • Partnership with local NGOs and youth networks
  • Includes mentorship from social innovation leaders
  • Special funding for community-impact projects
04 — Financial Accessibility

Flexible Options for Every Family

Beyond scholarships, NIIT offers a range of options to make payments manageable.

Monthly Plans

Pay your fees in convenient monthly installments.

Sponsorship

NIIT helps connect students with employer & community sponsors.

Work-Study

Earn while you study through campus and partner opportunities.

Early-Bird Discounts

Reduced rates for early applicants and full upfront payments.

Apply for a Scholarship Today

Scholarship applications are reviewed alongside admissions. Apply now to be considered for all available awards.
