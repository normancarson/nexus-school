Frequently Asked Questions

01

What is NIIT?

What does NIIT stand for and what do you do?

NIIT stands for Nexus Institute of Innovation & Technology. We are a hybrid learning facilitation and academic support institution based in Rwanda. We help students succeed in internationally recognized online degree programs by providing structured physical study spaces, mentorship, AI-enhanced learning support, and project-based coaching.

Is NIIT a university?

No. NIIT does not independently award academic degrees, diplomas, or accredited university qualifications. We operate as a modern facilitation center — your degree is issued by the accredited international university you enroll in, while NIIT provides the local support that helps you succeed.

Where is NIIT located?

NIIT is headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda. Visit our contact page for the exact address, directions, and visit booking.

02

How the Facilitation Model Works

What exactly does NIIT facilitate?

We facilitate your enrollment, study journey, and success in accredited online degree programs. You attend physical study sessions at our campus, get mentorship and AI training, and complete your coursework with the international university you're enrolled in.

Do I have to attend campus every day?

No — but regular attendance is highly encouraged. Our hybrid model includes a few scheduled facilitation sessions per week plus open study hours. Most students benefit from being on campus regularly to use the AI lab and study with peers.

Is the AI Lab included for everyone?

Yes — every NIIT student participates in the AI Learning Lab regardless of their primary pathway. It's part of the core NIIT experience.

03

International Degree Support

Which universities can I enroll in through NIIT?

NIIT facilitates enrollment with multiple accredited international online universities and learning platforms across business, IT, public health, social sciences, education, and more. Our admissions team will match you with the right institution based on your goals, budget, and qualifications.

Are the degrees recognized in Rwanda and globally?

Yes — we only facilitate enrollment in degrees from accredited international institutions whose qualifications are internationally recognized. We help you choose programs that match your long-term goals.

Do I pay tuition to NIIT or to the university?

Tuition is paid directly to your international university partner. NIIT charges a separate facilitation fee that covers our campus, mentorship, AI lab, and student services.

04

Learning Schedule

When do classes / facilitation sessions happen?

Facilitation sessions are scheduled throughout the week with both daytime and evening options to fit students who work or have other commitments. Your specific schedule depends on your program and cohort.

Can I work while I study at NIIT?

Yes — many of our students are working professionals. Our hybrid model is specifically designed to be flexible enough to combine with employment or entrepreneurship.

What if I miss a session?

Most sessions are recorded and you can catch up through our digital learning platform. Your mentor will also help you stay on track if you miss anything important.

05

Student Support

What kind of mentorship do I get?

You're matched with both an academic mentor and a career mentor. You'll have regular 1-on-1 sessions, group workshops, and access to office hours. See the mentorship structure →

Is there technical / IT support?

Yes — our team helps you with platform onboarding, software setup, and any technical issues you encounter throughout your studies.

What if I'm struggling with my coursework?

Reach out to your mentor immediately — they're trained to spot when students are struggling and provide tutoring, study plans, and additional resources to get you back on track.

Do you help with career placement?

Yes — through our employer network, alumni community, and career mentorship we help you with internships, projects, CV building, and job applications. Learn more →
